About Us : PULSE BUDDY

Pulse Buddy is a cutting-edge healthcare chatbot that integrates seamlessly with oximeter devices, providing users with personalized health insights, real-time monitoring, and actionable recommendations. By leveraging the power of artificial intelligence (AI) and medical device integration, Pulse Buddy revolutionizes remote health monitoring, empowering individuals to take control of their health from anywhere, at any time.

Features

Real-Time Monitoring

Pulse Buddy continuously tracks vital signs,including blood oxygen saturation (SpO2) levels and heart rate, providing users with up-to-the-minute health insights.

Personalized Health Guidance

Utilizing AI algorithms, Pulse Buddy offers personalized health recommendations based on individual health data, lifestyle factors, and medical history.

Comprehensive Reporting

Pulse Buddy generates comprehensive health reports summarizing trends, patterns, and anomalies in users' health data over time, facilitating informed decision-making and proactive healthcare management
